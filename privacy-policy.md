# Privacy Policy

Naam Jaap respects your privacy.

This application is a spiritual counter app designed to help users
count Naam Jaap using simple tapping and game-style interactions.

## Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
No login, registration, or user account is required.

## Data Storage
All counters and progress are stored locally on the user's device only.
No data is transmitted to any server.

## Permissions
- Notification permission (optional): Used only for reminder notifications.
- Internet permission: Required only if online images or links are used.

## Advertisements
Currently, this app does not display any advertisements.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use analytics, tracking tools, or tracking SDKs.
